# III-V Semiconductor Research
Conducting graduate research on exploring unconventional dielectrics for integration with III-V semiconductors. Focusing on the design and optimization of simulated and physical devices
<div style="height:10px;font-size:1px;">&nbsp;</div>

<img src="assets/research.png" height="200" alt="RCWA Simulation">
<div style="height:20px;font-size:1px;">&nbsp;</div>

* Modeled novel III-V Optoelectronic devices using RCWA
* Optimized device performance with various dielectrics in visible and near-infrared regime
* Patterned and grew devices on wafers using MBE and sputtering
* Charactatized grown devices to compare to simulations and literature
